About us

Built on documentation, not promises.

Summit Mineral Holdings exists to prove that African gold can move to global markets with the same integrity, paperwork, and discipline as bullion from anywhere else on earth.

Vision

To be Africa's most trusted bridge between responsible mines and the world's refineries.

Mission

Source, mine, and export precious minerals through fully documented, audited supply chains that strengthen the communities behind them.

Company history

A decade in the making.

  1. 2016

    Founded in Kampala as a licensed gold trading house serving East African artisanal producers.

  2. 2018

    First DRC buying centre opens in Bunia; ICGLR regional certification achieved.

  3. 2020

    Karamoja exploration licences granted; gravimetric processing plant commissioned in Moroto.

  4. 2022

    Refinery partnerships signed in Dubai and Mumbai; annual export volume passes 2 tonnes.

  5. 2024

    Miner-formalisation programme enrols its 1,000th artisanal miner across both regions.

  6. 2026

    Licence area expands to 612 km²; export reach grows to 17 destinations across 4 continents.

Core values

What we won't trade away.

Integrity of origin

Every gram we trade is traceable to a documented source. Chain-of-custody is not a marketing line for us - it is the product.

Discipline in compliance

We operate under OECD due-diligence guidance, LBMA responsible-sourcing principles, and ICGLR regional certification frameworks.

Partnership over transaction

We build decade-long relationships with miners, refiners, and buyers. Trust compounds faster than margin.

Communities first

Mining only earns its licence when the regions that host it become stronger - in income, health, and opportunity.

Corporate governance

Audited from boardroom to pit.

Board oversight

An independent board committee reviews sourcing, sanctions screening, and counterparty risk quarterly.

Third-party audit

Chain-of-custody audited annually by independent assessors against OECD Annex II risks.

KYC & AML

Every counterparty passes documented KYC, beneficial-ownership checks, and sanctions screening before first trade.

Whistleblower channel

A confidential, externally managed reporting line is open to employees, miners, and partners.
